Fighter Aircraft

F-35 Lightning II

The F-35 Lightning II is a family of single-seat, single-engine, all-weather stealth multirole fighters developed by Lockheed Martin.

  • Max Speed: Mach 1.6
  • Range: 1,200 miles
  • Ceiling: 50,000 ft
  • Armament: Internal weapons bay, external hardpoints
  • Stealth: Yes (Very Low Observable)
  • Variants: F-35A (CTOL), F-35B (STOVL), F-35C (CV)

F-22 Raptor

The F-22 Raptor is a single-seat, twin-engine, all-weather stealth tactical fighter aircraft developed by Lockheed Martin for the United States Air Force.

  • Max Speed: Mach 2.25
  • Range: 1,840 miles
  • Ceiling: 65,000 ft
  • Armament: Internal weapons bay, air-to-air missiles
  • Stealth: Yes (Very Low Observable)
  • Features: Supercruise, thrust vectoring

Su-57

The Sukhoi Su-57 is a Russian single-seat, twin-engine stealth multirole fighter aircraft developed by Sukhoi for the Russian Aerospace Forces.

  • Max Speed: Mach 2
  • Range: 2,175 miles
  • Ceiling: 65,000 ft
  • Armament: Internal weapons bay, air-to-air missiles
  • Stealth: Yes (reduced radar cross-section)
  • Features: Thrust vectoring, supercruise capability

Bombers

B-2 Spirit

The B-2 Spirit is an American heavy strategic bomber featuring low observable stealth technology designed to penetrate dense anti-aircraft defenses.

  • Range: 6,900 miles
  • Ceiling: 50,000 ft
  • Payload: 40,000 lbs
  • Stealth: Yes (Very Low Observable)
  • Crew: 2
  • Features: Flying wing design, all-aspect stealth

B-21 Raider

The B-21 Raider is an American heavy bomber under development by Northrop Grumman, designed to be the next-generation stealth bomber for the United States Air Force.

  • Range: Intercontinental (exact specifications classified)
  • Payload: Conventional and nuclear weapons
  • Stealth: Yes (Advanced Low Observable)
  • Crew: 2 (with optional unmanned operation)
  • Features: Flying wing design, advanced sensors

Tu-160

The Tupolev Tu-160 is a supersonic, variable-sweep wing heavy strategic bomber designed by the Tupolev Design Bureau in the Soviet Union.

  • Max Speed: Mach 2.05
  • Range: 7,600 miles
  • Ceiling: 49,200 ft
  • Payload: 88,000 lbs
  • Crew: 4
  • Features: Variable-sweep wing, supersonic capability

Military Drones/UAVs

MQ-9 Reaper

The MQ-9 Reaper is an armed, multi-mission, medium-altitude, long-endurance remotely piloted aircraft primarily used for intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance missions.

  • Endurance: 27+ hours
  • Range: 1,150 miles
  • Ceiling: 50,000 ft
  • Payload: 3,800 lbs
  • Armament: AGM-114 Hellfire missiles, GBU-12 Paveway II bombs

RQ-4 Global Hawk

The RQ-4 Global Hawk is a high-altitude, long-endurance unmanned aerial vehicle designed for intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance missions.

  • Endurance: 34+ hours
  • Range: 14,000 miles
  • Ceiling: 60,000 ft
  • Sensors: Synthetic aperture radar, electro-optical/infrared sensors
  • Features: Autonomous operation, all-weather capability

S-70 Okhotnik

The Sukhoi S-70 Okhotnik is a Russian stealth heavy unmanned combat aerial vehicle being developed by Sukhoi as a sixth-generation aircraft project.

  • Max Speed: Subsonic (exact specifications classified)
  • Range: 3,500+ miles (estimated)
  • Stealth: Yes (flying wing design)
  • Armament: Internal weapons bay
  • Features: Autonomous operation, potential "loyal wingman" capability

Military Helicopters

AH-64 Apache

The AH-64 Apache is an American twin-turboshaft attack helicopter with a tailwheel-type landing gear arrangement and a tandem cockpit for a crew of two.

  • Max Speed: 182 mph
  • Range: 300 miles
  • Ceiling: 21,000 ft
  • Armament: 30mm chain gun, Hellfire missiles, Hydra 70 rockets
  • Features: Target acquisition and designation system, night vision systems

CH-53K King Stallion

The CH-53K King Stallion is a heavy-lift cargo helicopter designed and produced by Sikorsky Aircraft for the United States Marine Corps.

  • Max Speed: 200 mph
  • Range: 530 miles
  • Payload: 36,000 lbs
  • Crew: 5 (2 pilots, 3 crew chiefs)
  • Features: Triple-hook external cargo system, fly-by-wire controls

Ka-52 Alligator

The Kamov Ka-52 Alligator is a Russian all-weather attack helicopter with a distinctive coaxial rotor system, side-by-side cockpit, and unique ejection system.

  • Max Speed: 186 mph
  • Range: 460 miles
  • Ceiling: 18,000 ft
  • Armament: 30mm cannon, anti-tank missiles, rockets
  • Features: Coaxial rotor system, ejection seats, all-weather capability

Future Air Combat Systems

Next-Generation Air Dominance

The future of air combat is being shaped by sixth-generation fighter programs, loyal wingman concepts, and increasingly autonomous systems that will redefine aerial warfare.

Key Developments:

  • Sixth-Generation Fighters: Programs like the U.S. Next Generation Air Dominance (NGAD), UK's Tempest, and Europe's FCAS aim to develop fighters with unprecedented stealth, speed, and AI integration.
  • Loyal Wingman Concept: Unmanned combat aerial vehicles designed to accompany manned fighters, extending capabilities while reducing risk to pilots.
  • Directed Energy Weapons: Laser and microwave weapons that could revolutionize air defense and offensive capabilities.
  • Hypersonic Platforms: Aircraft and missiles capable of sustained flight at Mach 5+ speeds, potentially rendering current air defense systems obsolete.

Expected Capabilities:

  • Advanced AI for autonomous operation and decision support
  • Optionally manned configurations
  • Adaptive cycle engines with unprecedented performance
  • Integrated sensor fusion across multiple platforms
  • Quantum radar and communications systems
  • Self-healing materials and structures
